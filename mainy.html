Questions
Frequently asked
Do I need any prior experience or a degree? +
Absolutely not. This course was designed for complete beginners. If you've ever used a phone or a laptop, you have enough to start. Oluwatosin built this course specifically for people who felt lost in tech — because she was one of them.
How long do I have access after payment? +
You have 12 months of full access from the date of payment. Your login will remain active for that period. After 12 months, access expires — so make sure to complete the course and download any resources within that window.
What if I fail the module quiz? +
No problem — you can retake the quiz as many times as you need. The 80% pass mark is there to keep you honest and ensure you're really absorbing the material, not to block you. Review the lesson, retake the quiz, and move forward.
Will I get a certificate at the end? +
Yes. You'll receive a final course completion certificate that you can add to your LinkedIn profile and CV. You'll also earn a shareable digital badge after every module — so you can show progress publicly before you even finish.
What tools will I need and are they free? +
Most tools used in this course have free versions. Microsoft Excel (or Google Sheets as an alternative), SQL Server Express (free), Tableau Public (free), and Power BI Desktop (free). You won't need to buy any paid software to complete the course.
Is the WhatsApp community included in both plans? +
The WhatsApp community is included in the Self-Paced + Community plan (₦50,000) only. The self-paced plan (₦35,000) gives you full course access but without the community and live Q&A sessions.
